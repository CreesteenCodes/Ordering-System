About Us

The Heart Behind Dimsum by Luna

Welcome to Dimsum by Luna, your home for freshly made, heartwarming dimsum. What began as a simple love for good food became our mission — to share authentic flavors that bring comfort and joy to every meal. Each dish is prepared with care, using quality ingredients and a touch of love — just like how you’d make it for family. With just a few taps, you can enjoy homemade goodness delivered right to your doorstep.

Our Vision

Dimsum by Luna aims to be everyone’s go-to spot for delicious, freshly made dishes that bring comfort and joy with every bite. We dream of making authentic flavors easy to enjoy anytime, anywhere—bringing restaurant-quality food right to your doorstep with just a few taps.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make ordering comfort dishes simple, fast, and satisfying. We’re dedicated to serving delicious, affordable, and freshly prepared meals while providing a smooth and welcoming online experience. At Dimsum by Luna, we bring together good food, warm service, and a touch of home in every order.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

You can order your favorite dimsum by browsing our menu, adding items to your cart, and checking out. Just choose your delivery address and payment method — it’s that easy!
We currently deliver within selected areas near our kitchen or partner locations. You can check if we deliver to your area by entering your address during checkout.
Delivery time usually depends on your location and order volume. On average, you can expect your food to arrive within 30 to 40 minutes after confirmation.
We currently accept online payments through GCash, Maya, and PayPal for your convenience. More payment options may be added soon!
If your order is incorrect, delayed, or missing, please reach out to our Support Center or message us directly through our contact page. We’ll be happy to help and make it right.
